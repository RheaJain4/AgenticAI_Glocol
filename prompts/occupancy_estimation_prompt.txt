=========================

Estimate occupancy using the following reasoning process:

   - This represents the primary source of occupancy.

2. Time-of-Day Adjustment
   Consider:
   - Morning commute
   - School hours
   - Business hours
   - Evening
   - Nighttime

3. Day-of-Week Adjustment
   Consider:
   - Weekday work/school activity
   - Weekend residential and recreational patterns

4. Infrastructure Adjustment
   Use nearby infrastructure only to estimate how people are distributed,
   NOT to blindly add occupants.

   Typical occupancy references:
   - Schools: ~300–800 students/staff during weekday school hours
   - Hospitals: ~100–500 staff/patients/visitors throughout the day
   - Transit Stations:
       • Rush hour: ~200–2000
       • Off-peak: ~50–200

5. Event Impact
   Consider whether the event severity is likely to slightly increase or
   decrease occupancy due to evacuation, sheltering, commuting disruption,
   or emergency response.
